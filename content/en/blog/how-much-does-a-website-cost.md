It's the first question almost every client asks me. Before talking about design or features, the budget is at the heart of every conversation.

That's completely normal. Building a website is an investment, and nobody wants to be hit with a surprise bill.

Here's what nobody tells you clearly: **prices vary enormously**, and understanding why helps you make the right choice.

## The three main categories

### The essential showcase website

For an SME or tradesperson who simply wants to be online with their information, services and a contact form, **a well-built showcase site costs between €800 and €1,500**.

This type of site meets 80% of small local business needs. It typically consists of 3 to 5 pages: home, services, about, contact.

**What's included:**
- Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Contact form
- Basic Google optimisation
- Hosting and maintenance

### The advanced showcase website

If your business needs more pages, a blog, a bilingual site or an elaborate visual identity, expect **between €1,200 and €2,500**.

This format suits businesses that want to truly stand out from local competitors and generate qualified leads via Google.

### The e-commerce website

Online shops are a different level of complexity entirely. Product catalogue, shopping cart, secure payment, stock management... **expect from €1,500 for a 20-product shop**.

## What really drives the price

### 1. Functional complexity

A site with online booking, a client portal or ERP integration costs more than a static showcase site. Every custom feature represents hours of development.

### 2. Content

If you provide all your texts and photos, the cost drops. If the developer needs to produce everything (SEO copywriting, photography, illustrations), the price rises.

### 3. Design

A bespoke design, created from scratch for your brand, costs more than an adapted template. But it guarantees a unique, professional visual identity.

### 4. Maintenance

A website needs upkeep. Hosting, security updates, backups, minor changes... Budget **a monthly fee of €30 to €80** depending on complexity.

## Pitfalls to avoid

### "Website for €99" offers

These offers exist and generally correspond to basic templates on platforms like Wix or Jimdo. You'll be limited in features, design and SEO. Hard to stand out with a site identical to hundreds of others.

### Vague quotes

A quote that just says "Website: €2,500" without specifying what's included is a red flag. Always ask for detail: number of pages, features, hosting included or not, maintenance, deadlines.
